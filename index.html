India Digital Asset Exchange

InDAX is a digital assets and cryptocurrency exchange platform which combines secure exchange, advanced trading, instant fiat transactions, and various other industry-specific modules. Entire ecosystem runs on high performance, scalable and robust infrastructure for a powerful platform which is cross-chain, cross-system, cross-industry, cross-application, and cross-device.

Current Issues in Exchanges

Poor Privacy Protection

Users do not have control of their own identity information.

No Liquidity

Liquidity is often absent in exchange orderbook.

Slow Trade Engine

Users often are unable to execute trades instantly hence losing trading opportunity.

Delayed Fiat Transactions

Users face issues in getting withdrawals on time.

Poor Support

Support is mostly irresponsive and not helpful in many cases.

High Fees

Most exchange charge very high fees as compared to international market.

No Exchange Audits

Lack of exchange assets audit lead its users in distrust.

Weak Security Systems

There is a lack of comprehensive security and identity management systems.

More Issues

No dividends, forks and airdrops support, low pairs of cryptocurrency,etc
